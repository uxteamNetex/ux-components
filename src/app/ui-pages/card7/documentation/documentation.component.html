Cards

ntx-card component provides a flexible and extensible content container with multiple variants and options. A card includes options for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contextual background colors, and powerful display options.

Card 7

Use ntx-card7 whenever you need to show in a card a one line title with a two lines description below, and a menu that displays list of options.
